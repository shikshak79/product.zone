**Canonical Reference · v1.1 · March 2026**

## Design Principles

**Fixed scope is the signature.**
Every offer has a defined scope, defined deliverables, and a defined end state. No ongoing retainers disguised as projects. No scope negotiation mid-engagement. The founder knows exactly what they are buying before they sign.

**Outcomes, not sessions.**
Pricing reflects the value of the outcome, not the number of sessions. Session count is a delivery detail, not a commercial variable.

**Weekly cadence is the baseline.**
All sprint-based products run in a weekly session rhythm by default. A fast-track option compresses the same scope into a shorter calendar window for founders who need speed. Same price, different pace.

**Decisions first, surfaces second.**
The entry product locks positioning decisions. Surfaces are only built once decisions are locked. This sequence is non-negotiable — building surfaces on fuzzy decisions is the failure mode the diagnostic exists to prevent.

**Risk reversal through scope, not price.**
The entry product is a complete, standalone outcome. The Full Sprint is a separate buying decision made after the entry product proves its value. Founders never commit to the full arc upfront.

## Product 0: X-RAY Diagnostic (Free)

### Purpose
Lead generation and routing. A founder completes the X-RAY and receives a diagnostic read of where their buying path breaks. The output creates awareness of the problem and routes them toward the right entry point.

### Format
Self-serve. No session required. Founder completes independently.

### Output
- Scorecard showing which buyer questions their surfaces answer
- Preliminary decision gap map
- Fix-first recommendation with routing to entry product or full sprint

### Entry criteria
Any post-product B2B software founder. No qualification required.

### Routing logic
- Decisions fuzzy, no surfaces built → Entry Product
- Decisions partially locked, surfaces exist but misaligned → Entry Product
- Decisions locked, surfaces built, motion not installed → Full Sprint

## Product 1: Positioning & Messaging Sprint (Entry Product)

### Price
€5,000

### Duration
- Standard: 3 weeks (one session per week)
- Fast track: 2 weeks (sessions in weeks 1 and 2, with interim async work)

### Purpose
Lock the positioning decisions that all sales surfaces depend on. Produce a messaging spine the founder can use immediately. Create the diagnostic baseline that makes the Full Sprint viable.

### What is included
**Sessions (3 total):**
- Session 1: Diagnostic — surface audit, decision gap map, buying path break identified
- Session 2: Positioning strategy — segment prioritisation, positioning options ranked by risk, bet selected
- Session 3: Decisions & messaging spine — 18 core decisions locked, messaging architecture complete, homepage spine draft

**Deliverables (in Notion workspace):**
- Buying path diagnostic report (scorecard + decision gap map, 20-question surface audit)
- Competitive landscape summary
- Segment morphology (target company + target role, now and next)
- Positioning strategy document (3 options ranked, selected bet with rationale)
- Decision ledger (18 decisions locked, in buyer language)
- Messaging spine (category, customer, alternative, difference, outcome, proof, first step)
- Homepage spine draft (hero + first scroll, copy-ready)
- Messaging repository (reusable copy blocks by buying stage)

**Handover:**
- Handover session (30 min): walkthrough of all Notion deliverables
- Figma assets linked in Motion where applicable

### What is not included
- Full homepage build
- Pitch deck
- Sales brief
- Demo script
- Outbound templates
- Any surface beyond the homepage spine

### Founder time commitment
~4 hours total across 3 weeks:
- Pre-work completion: ~1.5 hours
- Session attendance: 3 × 60–75 min
- Async review and feedback between sessions

### Entry criteria
- B2B software product with at least one paying customer or active pilot
- Founder-CEO available for all sessions
- Pre-work materials available (see Engagement Playbook)

### Exit state
Founder leaves with positioning locked and a messaging spine they can immediately apply to sales conversations, outbound, and LinkedIn. They have a clear view of what surfaces still need to be built — which becomes the natural entry point for the Full Sprint conversation.

### Transition to Full Sprint
The Full Sprint is a separate buying decision made after the Entry Product completes. It is not automatically bundled. The handover session includes a gap analysis showing what the Full Sprint would build on top of the locked decisions.

## Product 2: Buyability & Sales Motion Sprint (Full Sprint)

### Price
€10,000

### Duration
- Standard: 6 weeks (one session per week)
- Fast track: 4 weeks (sessions in weeks 1–4, compressed rhythm)

### Purpose
Take locked positioning decisions and build the complete surface set and sales motion. Produce handoff-ready assets a team can operate without the founder in the room.

### Prerequisite
Positioning decisions locked — either through the Entry Product or an equivalent prior engagement. The Full Sprint does not re-do positioning work. If decisions are not locked, the Entry Product must come first.

### What is included
**Sessions (6 total, building on Entry Product):**
- Session 4: Buyability decisions — full homepage architecture, all 18 buyer questions answered
- Session 5: Sales pitch — pitch deck built, founder practices delivery, revised until it lands
- Session 6: Sales motion — sales brief, demo script, objection → asset map, handover

**Deliverables (added to Notion workspace from Entry Product):**
- Full homepage copy (all sections, production-ready, answers 20 buyer questions)
- Pitch deck (Figma, complete)
- Sales brief (Day-1 reference for rep or agency)
- Demo script (5-minute arc, repeatable by anyone)
- Objection → asset map (by buying stage)
- Buying path documentation (how buyer moves from Land to Commit)
- Asset library (all surfaces in one place, version-controlled)

**Handover:**
- Handover session (60 min): full walkthrough, usage instructions, what to test next
- Figma assets linked in Motion
- Notion workspace complete and structured for ongoing use

### What is not included
- Ongoing content creation
- CRM setup or RevOps
- Paid media or lead generation execution
- Outbound campaign management

### Founder time commitment
~8 hours across 6 weeks:
- Session attendance: 6 × 60–75 min
- Async review and feedback between sessions
- Message testing encouraged but not enforced

### Entry criteria
- Entry Product completed (or equivalent — assessed case by case)
- Founder-CEO available for all sessions
- Willingness to test new messages between sessions

### Exit state
Founder leaves with a complete, aligned surface set and a documented sales motion that a rep, agency, or co-founder can operate independently. All assets version-controlled and accessible in Notion + Figma.

## Product 3: Stabilisation Retainer

### Price
€2,000/month

### Duration
6 weeks standard (2 billing months). Extendable by agreement.

### Purpose
After the Full Sprint, founders need time to test new positioning and surfaces in the real world. The Stabilisation Retainer provides structured support during this period — building a test plan, interpreting results, and adjusting assets as signal comes in. When positioning is stable, the next Sprint becomes viable.

### Format
- Bi-weekly 60-minute working session (2 sessions per month)
- Async support via Slack or WhatsApp between sessions
- Deliverable reviews and copy adjustments as needed

### What is included
- Test plan for new messaging and surfaces
- Interpretation of early results (qualitative + quantitative signals)
- Targeted adjustments to existing assets (not new builds)
- Decision ledger updates as positioning stabilises
- Readiness assessment for next Sprint

### What is not included
- New surface builds (homepage rebuild, new deck, etc.)
- Lead generation or outbound execution
- Weekly sessions (bi-weekly is the standard)

### Entry criteria
- Full Sprint completed
- At least one surface live and generating buyer interactions
- Founder actively running sales conversations

### Exit state
Positioning stable. Key signals positive. Clear hypothesis for what the next Sprint should address.
