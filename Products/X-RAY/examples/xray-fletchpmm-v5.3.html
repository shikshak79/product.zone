04
Opportunities to strengthen
Light Refinement — Not a Major Rebuild
This homepage is already strong. Three targeted additions would close the gaps:
→ Name the Alternative: Add a section addressing "Why Fletch vs. DIY / Freelancer / Agency / Internal Hire?" — preempt the comparison question.
→ Quantify Cost of Waiting: Add urgency — "Every month with unclear positioning costs you X% conversion" or "Deals stall when buyers can't explain your value."
→ Add Risk Reversal: Consider explicit language about what happens if the positioning doesn't land — even a light "we iterate until you're confident" would help.
