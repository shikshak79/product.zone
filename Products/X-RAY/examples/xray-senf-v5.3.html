04
Opportunities to strengthen
Light Refinement — Not a Major Rebuild
This homepage is already strong. Four targeted additions would close the gaps:
→ Add Urgency Framing: Quantify the cost of manual engagement. "Every project without structured engagement costs X hours of consolidation" or "RFPs without digital methodology win Y% less often."
→ Swap Vanity for Outcome Metrics: Replace "250+ projects" with customer outcomes: "60% less time from feedback to report" or "X hours saved per project on average."
→ Add Role Titles to Testimonials: "Dr. Julian Petrin, Partner" or "Michael Frehn, Project Lead" — let buyers see proof from their role.
→ Show Indicative Pricing: Even "Starting at €X per project" or tier names (Starter/Professional/Enterprise) would help budget conversations happen before the demo.
