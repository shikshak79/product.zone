After the Sprint, founders need time to test new positioning in the real world. This is structured support for that period.
Format
Standard duration
6 weeks (2 billing months)
Cadence
Bi-weekly sessions + async
What's included
  • Test plan. Structured approach to testing new messaging and surfaces with real buyers.
  • Signal interpretation. Read qualitative and quantitative signals from live conversations and surfaces.
  • Asset adjustments. Targeted revisions to existing assets as signal comes in. Not new builds.
  • Decision ledger updates. Refine locked decisions as real-world feedback improves them.
  • Sprint readiness assessment. Clear hypothesis for what the next Sprint should address.
Not included
New surface builds (homepage rebuild, new deck) · Lead generation or outbound execution · Weekly sessions (bi-weekly is standard)
Exit state
Positioning stable. Key signals positive. Clear hypothesis for what the next Sprint should address and when.
